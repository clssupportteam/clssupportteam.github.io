Handling Objections

Here are some common client objections and a few suggestions on how to handle them.

If the Client advises they are not the Exec

"Ok what I will do is send the hub link to you, for you to share with the the decision maker. However the Executor will need to call back (please leave full booking notes with estate information if this meeting is owned). Information needs to be reiterated on 2nd call with the decision maker."

Client plans to DIY

That’s great, are you aware of what is involved, I could still arrange for someone to contact you to help you understand all your options and the cost of Probate "

The Solicitors hold the Will, so I thought I needed to use them

"If they are not named as the Exec and are only holding the Will, ultimately its up to the Exec how they want to proceed."

Own Solicitor

"HAVE YOU INSTRUCTED THE SOLICITOR? - The reason I ask is if you have not yet instructed then we can arrange a meeting with one of the CLS consultants so that you can do a comparison with service and fee structure"

It’s a Simple Estate

"Good! That’s going to help you a lot, however sometimes complexities may arise. You may benefit from having a meeting with our consultant who can provide you with relevant information and leave you with a guaranteed fixed fee quote."

I'm not Ready

"Of course we understand" - send hub link and pipeline where possible. So that there is continuity for the client
